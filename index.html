DermLens is a computer vision research project developed at Shen Dermatology (Temecula, California) focused on quantifying visual markers of treatment progression during Shen Cocktail, a combination topical immunotherapy used to treat basal cell carcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma.

The project aims to develop deep learning-based image analysis methods that assist dermatologists in longitudinal treatment monitoring by measuring and statistically analyzing macroscopic visual biomarkers associated with tissue response, including inflammation, necrosis, and regrowth, as observed in clinical photographs collected throughout therapy.

By transforming qualitative clinical observations into reproducible quantitative metrics, DermLens seeks to support objective treatment assessment and improve understanding of visual patterns associated with successful topical immunotherapy.
