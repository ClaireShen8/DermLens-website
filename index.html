DermLens is a dermatology-focused research initiative building trustworthy, clinically useful computer vision tools. We study lesion segmentation, multimodal diagnosis, and explainability with a focus on real-world deployment constraints. Our work bridges data curation, model design, and human-centered evaluation to support earlier detection and improved care.
