SC-8000 is a longitudinal clinical imaging dataset comprising over 8,000 anonymized images of basal cell carcinoma (BCC) and squamous cell carcinoma (SCC) treatment sites before and during therapy with the Shen Cocktail, a combination topical immunotherapy and non-surgical intervention for the treatment of skin cancers. Images are acquired at baseline (prior to biopsy and treatment) and at regular two-week intervals across a 30-application treatment course, capturing immune response and tissue healing.

Collected over nearly two decades (2008-present) at Shen Dermatology, SC-8000 represents one of the largest single-therapy, longitudinal dermatology image datasets to date. The dataset enables analysis of time-dependent visual biomarkers--inflammation, tissue breakdown, and reconstruction--and is purpose-built for computer vision research. SC-8000 serves as the foundational dataset for DermLens.
