DermLens Dataset aggregates dermoscopy, clinical photography, and patient metadata with biopsy-confirmed labels. Access is granted for research under a data use agreement.

Overview

The dataset includes multiple acquisition devices, longitudinal follow-ups, and clinician notes for contextual diagnosis. We prioritize balanced representation across age, sex, and skin tone.

  • Images: 58,000 dermoscopy + 22,000 clinical photos
  • Labels: 14 primary diagnostic categories, 62 subtypes
  • Splits: Patient-level train/val/test with temporal holdout

Access

Submit a request describing your study, IRB approval (if applicable), and data security plan. We provide a standardized DUA and a model card with recommended evaluation protocols.
